    Survival/Slimefun

    Do you enjoy playing Survival? On our server, you'll find a large survival world that we strive to update regularly to the latest version of Minecraft. You can also enhance your experience with a large selection of perks, such as residences and shops.

    Minigames

    Have you had enough of building and survival and want to try something different? On our server, you'll find a variety of minigames which you can play by yourself or with a group of friends!

    Skyblock/Oneblock

    Are you an experienced survival player and you want to try something more? Try our fantastic Oneblock/Skyblock!

    Economy

    Not a fan of the classic survival and looking for something more? On our server, you'll find a great economy, which has been developing since 2017! You can earn money through work or competitions and buy loads of stuff with in-game money, whether is it's items in the shop, houses or items in auctions.
